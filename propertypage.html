PROPERTY DETAILS

Approved tenants are asked for their property details which are entered into our database for administration purposes.

Type the property details into the following fields to update our online database.

POSTCODE:

TENANT_ID:

ADDRESS:

REPAIR_ID:


If you do not know your postcode / eircode please click here to search for it.

If the details you selected do not enter into our database you have probably entered the wrong data type or you have selected a postcode which exists already in our database. Each property is given a unique postcode. If the same postcode is inserted into the database and a record already exists with this postcode, your information will not be entered into the database. To check and see existing postcode's in our database, click on the 'VIEW EXISTING PROPERTY DETAILS IN OUR DATABASE' link below.

VIEW EXISTING PROPERTY DETAILS IN OUR DATABASE

Your details you selected may not have worked also because you have selected a tenant id or a repairs id which is not in our database. To check and see if your tenant id or repair id is in our database click on the 'VIEW EXISTING TENANT DETAILS IN OUR DATABASE' link or the 'VIEW EXISTING REPAIRS DETAILS IN OUR DATABASE' link.

VIEW EXISTING TENANT DETAILS IN OUR DATABASE
VIEW EXISTING REPAIRS DETAILS IN OUR DATABASE
