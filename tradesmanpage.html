TRADESMAN DETAILS

It is important that we have the right tradesman with the right skills carrying out our repairs to our properties. This insures that the repairs are done to the highest standard possible. Please select the tradesman and equipment you need to carry out your repairs.

Type the tradesman details into the following fields to search our online database.

If the details you selected do not enter into our database you have probably entered the wrong data type or you have selected a tradesman id which exists already in our database. Each tradesman is given a unique tradesman id. If the same tradesman id is inserted into the database and a record already exists with this tradesman id, your information will not be entered into the database. To check and see existing tradesman id's in our database, click on the 'VIEW EXISTING TRADESMAN DETAILS IN OUR DATABASE' link below.

VIEW EXISTING TRADESMAN DETAILS IN OUR DATABASE

Your details you selected may not have worked also because you have selected a equipment id which is not in our database. To check and see if your equipment id is in our database click on the 'VIEW EXISTING EQUIPMENT DETAILS IN OUR DATABASE' link.
